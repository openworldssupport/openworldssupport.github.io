Getting Started

Two Ways to Participate

Join as a passenger for a seamless booking experience, or apply as staff to help run the operation.

✈️ Fly as a Passenger

Create an account, search available flights, and book your seat. Receive boarding information, track your flight live, and enjoy a realistic airline experience from departure to arrival.

  • Search & book flights on 15+ routes
  • Receive boarding passes
  • Track flights live on the status board
  • View booking history & upcoming trips
  • Get real-time delay & gate notifications
Browse flights

🏢 Join as Staff

Apply for a staff role, complete the required training, and operate flights as part of our ground crew, cabin crew, flight crew, or operations team. Every flight needs a full team.

  • Multiple departments & roles available
  • Structured training & certifications
  • Manage flights, passengers & operations
  • Issue announcements & flight updates
  • Earn promotions & senior permissions
View open roles

🎓 Training Academy

Every staff member must complete the appropriate training before receiving operational permissions. Our academy covers everything from safety procedures to advanced flight management.

  • 6 structured training modules
  • Lessons, exams & assessments
  • Progress saved to your account
  • Certifications upon completion
  • Instructor-approved practical training

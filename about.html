About OpenWorlds Aviation

A realistic aviation roleplay community built on a shared passion for flight simulation and immersive teamwork.

⚠️

Important Disclaimer — Please Read

OpenWorlds Aviation is entirely fictional and non-commercial. It is a roleplay community for entertainment purposes only.

This website simulates airline and airport operations within a flight simulator environment. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representative of any real airline, airport, aviation authority, air traffic control service, aircraft manufacturer, or any real-world commercial aviation entity. All flights, routes, schedules, flight numbers, bookings, and operational data shown on this website are entirely fictional. No real travel occurs, no real tickets are issued, and no real money changes hands. Any resemblance to real flight numbers, airlines, or routes is coincidental and used solely for realistic roleplay immersion. This community is run by volunteers for the enjoyment of flight simulation enthusiasts. Participation is free.

Our Story

Built by Aviation Enthusiasts, for Aviation Enthusiasts

OpenWorlds Aviation started as a small group of flight simulator enthusiasts who wanted more than just flying solo. We wanted the full airline experience — departures boards, boarding announcements, crew coordination, and the feeling of a real commercial operation.

Today, our community brings together hundreds of members who play different roles in a simulated airline ecosystem. Passengers book flights through our website, ground crew manage boarding, pilots operate the aircraft in-simulator, and our operations team coordinates everything from the virtual control centre.

Every flight we operate is a collaborative performance — and every member contributes to making it feel real.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by these core principles.

Realism & Immersion

We strive to simulate a genuine airline experience. Every procedure, announcement, and interaction should feel like the real thing — because that's what makes it exciting.

Teamwork & Community

Aviation is inherently a team effort. We value collaboration, communication, and supporting each other — whether you're a new passenger or a senior captain.

Learning & Development

Our structured training programme helps members grow. We celebrate progress, support newcomers, and encourage everyone to develop their skills over time.

Respect & Inclusion

We welcome everyone regardless of age, background, or experience level. Respect for all community members is non-negotiable.

Safety First

Even in a roleplay context, we take safety seriously — both in how we operate and in how we protect our members' wellbeing within the community.

Fun & Entertainment

At the end of the day, this is a hobby. We're here to enjoy the simulation, have fun with others, and create memorable moments together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real airline?

No. OpenWorlds Aviation is entirely fictional. It is a roleplay simulation community that uses a flight simulator to recreate the airline experience for entertainment. No real flights, real tickets, or real travel is involved.

Is it free to join?

Yes, completely free. OpenWorlds Aviation is a non-commercial community run by volunteers. There are no fees, subscriptions, or purchases required at any point.

What flight simulator do you use?

Community members operate within Roblox simulation environments. Our operations are coordinated on our Discord server alongside this website.

Do I need experience to join?

No experience required. Whether you want to be a passenger or apply for a staff role, our training academy provides everything you need to get started.

How do I become a staff member?

Visit the Careers page to see open roles, then apply via our application process. Once accepted, you'll complete the required training modules before receiving your permissions.

Where can I get support?

Join our Discord server for live support, or email us at opwnworldssupport@gmail.com.

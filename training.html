How Training Works

All staff members must complete the required training modules for their role before receiving operational permissions. Courses contain lessons, knowledge checks, and where noted, practical exams. Progress is automatically saved to your local profile. Certified completions are reviewed and approved by an instructor before permissions are granted. This is a roleplay training system — all content relates to simulator-based aviation operations only.

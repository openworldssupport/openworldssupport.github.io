Community Guidelines

We're committed to building a safe, inclusive, and welcoming community for all creators and players.

Respect & Kindness

Treat all community members with respect. We have zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or hateful content.

Privacy & Security

Protect your personal information and respect others' privacy. Never share or request sensitive data.

Original Content

Create original content and respect intellectual property rights. Plagiarism and copyright infringement are prohibited.

Safety First

Keep our platform safe by reporting harmful content and following all community rules and policies.

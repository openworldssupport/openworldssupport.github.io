Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Department: Executive Leadership Branch

The Chief Operating Officer helps shape internal operating rhythm, contributor coordination, planning flow, and execution discipline across OpenWorlds.

Responsibilities

  • Support internal operations and cross-team coordination
  • Improve contributor workflows and execution follow-through
  • Help leadership maintain clarity across ongoing initiatives

Required Skills

  • Operational leadership and organizational thinking
  • Strong communication and prioritization ability
  • Experience coordinating teams or systems

Preferred Skills

  • Startup, product, community, or online organization operations experience

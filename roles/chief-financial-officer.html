Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Department: Executive Leadership Branch

The Chief Financial Officer helps frame financial planning, stewardship thinking, budget structure, and sustainability guidance for OpenWorlds.

Responsibilities

  • Support financial planning and sustainability thinking
  • Help structure responsible budget and resource considerations
  • Provide financial clarity for leadership decisions

Required Skills

  • Financial planning or strategic budgeting experience
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Ability to communicate clearly around constraints and tradeoffs

Preferred Skills

  • Startup finance, nonprofit finance, or operational budgeting experience

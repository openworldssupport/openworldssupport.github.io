Chief Technical Officer (CTO)

Department: Executive Leadership Branch

The Chief Technical Officer provides senior technical leadership across platform architecture, engineering direction, technical standards, and long-range system planning.

Responsibilities

  • Define technical vision and architectural priorities
  • Guide engineering quality, scalability, and system decision-making
  • Align technology planning with platform growth goals

Required Skills

  • Strong engineering leadership background
  • Experience designing technical systems and roadmaps
  • Clear communication across technical and non-technical groups

Preferred Skills

  • UGC, platform, web infrastructure, or game technology leadership experience
